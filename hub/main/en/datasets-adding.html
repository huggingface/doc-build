Datasets outside a namespace

Datasets outside a namespace are maintained by the Hugging Face team on GitHub. Unlike the naming convention used for community datasets (username/dataset_name or org/dataset_name), datasets outside a namespace can be referenced directly by their name (e.g. glue).
