Change Organization or Account

Hugging Face Inference Endpoints uses your Hugging Face user for authentication. This allows you to either use your personal account or your organization account to deploy Inference Endpoints. You can switch between the two by clicking on the “organization/user” dropdown in the top right corner of the page. The dropdown will only show organizations and accounts, who have an active PLAN. Check out Access the solution to learn more.

Select the organization you want to switch to. If you don’t have access to any organization, you can create a new one by clicking on the “Create Organization” button.
